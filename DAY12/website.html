HIS ORIGIN

The viral video was originally captured during the Madhya Pradesh elections in 2018. It came into the spotlight as a response to BJP's claim that the state's roads were superior to America's. Bhupendra Jogi became a household name due to his candid conversation with the Lallantop team.During the interaction, Bhupendra was asked for his name and the names of places he had visited in the US. His response was simple yet unforgettable. He introduced himself as Bhupendra Jogi, and this marked the birth of an internet sensation. For years, the video remained relatively dormant, until now.
